About the App

This app enables users to explore a wide range of medicinal plants and their applications in Ayurveda. The Ayurveda Plants Explorer app serves as a comprehensive digital guide, bringing the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda directly to your fingertips. It offers detailed information on numerous medicinal plants, including their health benefits, uses, and traditional applications. Featuring a user-friendly interface, the app allows users to search, explore, and gain knowledge about each plant with ease. It is an invaluable resource for students, practitioners, and anyone interested in natural healing. By integrating modern technology with traditional knowledge, the app promotes holistic wellness and assists users in incorporating Ayurvedic remedies into their daily routines.

How to Use

Click on any plant box in the "Explore Ayurveda Plants" section to see its full details, including a 3D model, benefits, advantages, disadvantages, and traditional uses.

Use the navigation links in the header to jump to Home, Contact, or open these informative pop-ups.

Use the search bar to quickly find a specific plant by name (functionality coming soon!).
